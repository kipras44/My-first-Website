Going to the cinema.

In 2022-2023, I watched an abundance of entertaining movies at the cinema

And I am here to try and convince to you go to the cinema more often.

Cinema is the most beautiful fraud in the world.-Jean Luc Godard.

Over the last year, I have gone to the cinema roughly 20 times, and have seen some really incredible movies. I will admit, I have been absolutely spoiled for choice, and the fact that my girlfriend enjoys going to the cinema as much as I do has been a massive blessing. Whether you're after the hot new block buster, or want to catch up on some classics, the cinemas of London are happy to provide.
